Marketing Services FAQ

What's it like working with Digital Elevator?

We prioritize services that have maximum business impact, while also focusing on services that lay the foundation for more expansive marketing campaigns. Strategies and execution are based on best practices as well as conversations with you on the areas of your business that are most important to your growth goals.

How do you measure the impact of your services?

We set up monthly reporting and review it on recurring meetings to revisit the performance and direction of our work. Upon the onset of new engagements, we discuss KPIs with our clients to determine what is most important to them so we are aligned.

What tech stacks are you familiar with?

We have experience & expertise across enterprise development, analytics & digital marketing software platforms including all Google products, WordPress, HubSpot, Salesforce, AI & LLMs, Shopify, SEMRush, Ahrefs, Figma, LinkedIn, and more...

How do you use PhDs or SMEs in your projects?

We utilize PhDs across various aspects of our marketing from SEO, content writing, editing, and strategy. We bring in subject matter experts including PhDs, MDs, or specialized writers based on our clients’ area of expertise.

What's your philosophy on marketing?

Our marketing philosophy is aligned with how consumers interact with brands. Today’s buying cycle demands that brands are active at every stage of the buyer journey. From problem identification and solution exploration to requirements gathering, supplier selection, and purchase decisions, we craft strategies that encompass the entire sales process.
